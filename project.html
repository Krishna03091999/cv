2. Pig Game

  1. It 's just a fun game that involves 2 player and a dice.
  2. created using HTML, CSS, JS.
  3. Rules of the game are mentioned below.
    • The game has 2 player, playing in rounds
    • In each turn a player rolls a dice as many times as he wishes. Each results is added to the round score
    • BUT if the player rolls to 1, all his round score gets lost. After that it's next player turn
    • A player can choose to HOLD which means that his round score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that it's next player turn.
    • The player who reaches to 100 points first wins the game.
